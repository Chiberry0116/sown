SOWN Career Development Network is dedicated to equipping university students and recent graduates with critical career, entrepreneurial, and leadership skills through capacity building, mentorship, innovation, and empowerment programmes. We exist to reduce youth unemployment, foster self-reliance, and create pathways for inclusive growth, with a special focus on advancing young women who face greater barriers to success.

To build a generation of empowered, innovation, and self-reliant young people equipped with the skills, confidence, and opportunities to thrive in the digital economy and drive sustainable development in Nigeria and beyond.
