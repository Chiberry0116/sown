2025 SOWN Empowerment Workshop Report

Thriving Beyond Graduation: Skills, Strategy & Self-Reliance in Challenging Times
Empowering UNEC students, SOWN organized a workshop on the 9th of May 2025 for undergraduate students.In today’s unpredictable world, thriving after graduation requires real-world skills, smart strategy, and self-reliance.This dynamic session brought together thought leaders, entrepreneurs, and career experts to equip students with the tools to succeed beyond the classroom—no matter what the future holds. Participants explored both technical and soft skills essential for today’s job market, including digital tools, communication, entrepreneurship, and leadership. The workshop encouraged continuous learning and showed students how to build competencies that make them globally competitive. The Outcome Graduating students walked away empowered, equipped, and energized—ready not just to enter the world, but to thrive in it.Thriving isn't about waiting for the world to change—it's about being ready when it does.
