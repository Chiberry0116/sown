FOUNDER

Prof. Chinwe R. Okoyeuzu

Prof. Chinwe R. Okoyeuzu is a distinguished Professor of Finance at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka(Enugu Campus). Over the years, she has taught and mentored numerous undergraduate and postgraduate students, many of whom have gone on to thrive in academia, business, and professional practice. Her academic contribution are widely recognized, with several publications in reputable national and international journals. Beyond her remarkable academic career, Prof. Okoyeuzu is also a life coach and a dedicated mentor, offering guidance and support to students navigating both academic and personal challenges. Her passion for establish School Over What Next (SOWN) Career Development Network, a foundation commited to preparing young people for life beyond the classroom.
