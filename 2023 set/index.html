2023 SOWN Empowerment Workshop Report

Report on the SOWN Empowerment Workshop for Graduating Students School Over What Next (SOWN), in collaboration with the Department of Banking and Finance, University of Nigeria Enugu Campus, successfully held a two-day empowerment workshop for graduating students on the 9th and 10th of February, 2024 at the CBN Complex, UNEC, beginning at 10:00 AM daily.
Theme: Empowering Dreams: Embracing Growth Opportunities and Possibilities in the World
Sub-Themes: Overcoming Self-Limiting Beliefs:
Adopting a Winning Mentality for Success Navigating the New World of Opportunities: Emerging Trends and Demanding Skill Sets The workshop brought together resource persons and facilitators who shared insights on employment-focused opportunities and postgraduate study options, equipping students with the tools to confidently navigate life after graduation. Students benefited greatly from the sessions as they gained practical knowledge on overcoming self-doubt, identifying growth opportunities, and adapting to the emerging world of work. The interactive format allowed participants to ask questions, reflect on their personal journeys, and network with peers and mentors. The program was highly impactful, leaving participants inspired, better prepared, and motivated to embrace the next phase of their lives with confidence and clarity. SOWN remains committed to empowering young people with the skills, mindset, and guidance needed to thrive in a rapidly changing world.
